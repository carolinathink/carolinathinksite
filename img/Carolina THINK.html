Local Events

Here are all of our upcoming events:

Andriy Rusyn

Andriy is senior studying computer science with a passion for entrepreneurship and all things startup. He has spent the past 3 summers at various internships in San Francisco, where he was first exposed to the creative problem solving opportunities in entrepreneurship, and he is excited to help spread the culture of entrepreneurship around the UNC community.

Andrew Skinner

Andrew is a Business and International Studies major with a passion for community-based social initiatives. He is currently a marketing intern with CUBE, UNC’s social initiative incubator, and plans to research entrepreneurship in developing economies through his studies. Other interests include jazz, pottery, and the outdoors.

Izel Truesdale

Izel is a pre-business major and Spanish minor with a passion for entrepreneurship and start-up culture. Prior to becoming a student at UNC, he worked various jobs ranging from high-end retail to working for a direct marketing firm, to managing and doing A&R work for a local independent record label. Furthermore, Izel is currently working on a start-up company of his own and is excited about spreading the entrepreneurial spirit around campus to others.

Mason Lantay

Mason is a Management Studies and Psychology student who joined the THINK Admin team in the Fall of 2015. Experienced in applying psychological research and creating evidence based plans for development, Mason is founder and CEO of a venture called R.Ë.A. which makes it easier to find the best information available. Mason is passionate about fostering an innovative mindset and a vibrant community of entrepreneurs at UNC.

Samantha Davis

Samantha is a senior communication studies major with minors in creative writing and entrepreneurship, and has experience doing marketing and communications for several local startups. Her goal is to help introduce students in the UNC community to entrepreneurship and to spread the word about all the opportunities available to young entrepreneurs in the area.

Edward Leavy

Ed Leavy is a senior from Southport, CT who is studying Global Studies and Comm. His interests include international business and microfinance.

Andrew Neiswender

Andrew is a German major with an interest in nascent creative endeavors. He has been involved in several small ventures in Chapel Hill and is currently representing a music label for the artist, Well$. He is hoping to play a role in student outreach to bring more attention to innovations happening on and around campus.

Nic Byron

Nic Byron is a Sophomore Computer Science and Business double major. He currently works at the Apple Store at Southpoint as a service technician and is also involved with the Environmental Affairs Committee here at Carolina. Nic hopes to one day work on a start up in the craft beer industry.

Destiny Harrell

Destiny is a computer science and business double major. She got interested in entrepreneurship by watching her mom start her own business. She has a desire to start her own business in the next few years.

Eric Peden

Eric is a senior computer science major at UNC with a passion for creativity and entrepreneurship. He has worked as a software developer the last two summers in Durham and is passionate about the possibilities computers offer to entrepreneurs. He is excited to spread the entrepreneur spirit to the UNC student body.
