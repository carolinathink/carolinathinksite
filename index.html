Carolina THINK

Carolina THINK is UNC’s Entrepreneurship Club. We help students from a wide range of academic backgrounds develop entrepreneurship and innovation as a set of core life skills. In addition to hosting our own panels, workshops, and other events, we also connect students to the Triangle’s vibrant entrepreneurship scene by providing rides to local meetups and building THINKternship teams that work with area startups. We are an inclusive and supportive community which helps individuals develop their skills and ideas.

Find Out More

THINK Thursdays

THINK Thursdays are a weekly recurring event where the Carolina THINK community meets to pursue ideas, develop skills and get insight into the future of entrepreneurship. Past events have included panels, workshops and speakers covering a wide range of topics from Virtual Reality and Predictive Analytics to Pitching and Networking. THINK Thursdays are held every Thursday at 7:30 p.m. at the 1789 Venture Lab, 173 E. Franklin St., Chapel Hill.

Learn More

THINKternships

We believe that hands-on learning is the best way to develop entrepreneurial skills - but at the same time we realize that finding these opportunities can be a challenging and stressful experience. THINK connects teams of 3-4 students with local startups who will work on a meaningful project over the course of the semester while being mentored by student and professional advisors on campus.

Rides with THINK

Since the Triangle is one of the nation’s leading entrepreneurship hubs, there are multiple meetups, pitch parties, and other events happening every week. Because so many exciting things are happening in the area all the time and students don’t have reliable access to transportation, Carolina THINK helps its members take advantage of these opportunities. Every week, THINK will be coordinating rides to an Entrepreneurship event out in the local community.
